Steadfast Community

"Here For a Good Time, Not a Long Time"


Interested in becoming a first-time homeowner? Seeking privacy from unruly neighbors? Need a warm and cozy retreat from the busyness of our modern world? A home in Centralia may be right up your alley! (There are in fact no alleys left in Centralia.)

Only five residents remain in Centralia. They exist as the town's sole remaining ambassadors, stewards of the toxic land. The city is designated as a Borough by the Pennsylvania Commonwealth, and as such is run by a borough Council that sees to its upkeep.

Fans of volunteer work can participate in the Centralia Cleanup Day that takes place annually. Any person looking to have a significant impact on their local community would feel at home among these steadfast locals.

Nearly all houses in the town have been either condemned and demolished or destroyed by fire and sinkholes, so there is plenty of land to choose from. For those wishing to live off the grid and are no longer interested in the use of zip codes, it is important to note that residences in Centralia no longer have a zip code as it was revoked by the U.S. Postal Service.
