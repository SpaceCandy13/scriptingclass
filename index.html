An Invitation to the
Morbidly Curious


Dark tourism has been growing increasingly popular over the past decade and mystery lovers from all over the globe are seeking out strange and eerie locations to explore.

The juxtaposition of emptiness in a world so full of life and movement creates a morbid fascination in those who wish to experience the darker parts of our worldâ€™s history. This interest in the macabre attracts many to the hauntingly still forests and decaying ghost town of Centralia Pennsylvania, The Perpetual Flame.

The sight of one of the most devastating disasters in North American history, Centralia is located near the very center of Pennsylvania. It sits atop an expansive coal mine that, in 1962, caught fire and has never stopped burning.

The now abandoned town remains in ruin, reclaimed by nature and forgotten by time, making it a fascinating place for any dark tourist.

If you are one of these ever-curious individuals, then look no further than Centralia Pennsylvania as your next spooky destination.
