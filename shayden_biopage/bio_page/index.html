MEET THE ARTIST

Shayden Coburn is a first-year student in the Multimedia Production program at Lethbridge College. After falling in love with comic book and manga art styles, Shayden was inspired to begin creating her own stories and soon discovered a broad field of mediums that she wanted to work with. Sketching and digital art soon turned to comic book and video game design.

Shayden favors interactive narration because it puts the audience in control, and in doing so enables the author to create a narrative environment that simulates the emotional weight of firsthand experiences. Drawing inspiration from the creative storytelling methods used by game companies such as Square Enix, Telltale Games, and Bandai Namco, Shayden wishes to use her stories to express the importance of mental health, emotional growth, and found family.
